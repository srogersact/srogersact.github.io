Using Quotations, Version 2

If the speech tag is before the quote...

Add a comma after the speech tag and before the quotation marks, like this:

Grandmother asked JimThis is the speech tag., "Are you afraid of snakes?This is the quote."

Be sure to capitalize the first word in the quote.

Problem 1

If the speech tag is after the quote...

Ordinarily, add a comma after the quote and before the speech tag.

I am afraid of snakesThis is the quote.," Jim told GrandmotherThis is the speech tag..

Problem 2

Don't add a comma if the quote is followed by a question mark...

Are you afraid of snakes?This is the quote." Grandmother asked JimThis is the speech tag..
