Capitals in Titles

Most of the words in a book's title should be capitalized.
You should always capitalize these words:
  • the first word,
  • the last word,
  • and almost every word in between.
Some words in a book's title should NOT be capitalized.
If these words are not the first word or the last word in a title, they should not be capitalized:
  • articles (a, an, the),
  • conjunctions (such as and, or, and but),
  • most prepositions (such as to, for, and from.
