Compound Sentences

Learning about compound sentences is an easy place to begin analyzing and composing sentences.

  • When you analyze a sentence, you break it into meaningful parts.
  • When you compose a sentence, you join meaningful parts together to make a complete sentence.

Here is a compound sentence:

Egypt is a country in Africa, and Greece is a country in Europe.

It can easily be analyzed as two sentences. You simply change the comma into a period and remove the conjunction: "And" is a conjunction.

Egypt is a country in Africa. Greece is a country in Europe.
